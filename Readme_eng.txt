About:

 Lodestar - arcade game to subjects of the cosmic fighting. She consists of five levels, which do not similar the friend on friend by its playing process. In them to be kept the elements of the different plays such as Asteroids, Lander and others. On plot you ordinary warrior to empires Lodestar, which there is get through many obstacles and rescue the world from extraterrestrial invader. All that occurs on screen during play is closely connected with plot.

   Lodestar - a game written on FreeBasic. Library was used For  FMOD for play music.
